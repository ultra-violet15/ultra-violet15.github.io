ABOUT US

Tucked away in the heart of the English countryside, The Velvet Rose Inn offers a luxurious retreat where timeless charm and modern comfort intertwine. Perfect for romantic getaways, peaceful escapes, or celebrating life’s special moments, our inn invites you to relax, unwind, and savor the beauty of your surroundings.

Why Choose The Velvet Rose Inn?

  • Elegant Accommodations: Each room is thoughtfully designed with a touch of sophistication, featuring plush bedding, vintage décor, and modern amenities to ensure your ultimate comfort.
  • Exquisite Breakfasts: Begin your day with a delightful breakfast crafted with care, showcasing seasonal, locally sourced ingredients. Think warm pastries, farm-fresh eggs, and aromatic coffee that stirs the soul.
  • A Serene Setting: Nestled amidst rolling hills with a tranquil garden, our inn is the perfect escape from the bustle of everyday life.
  • Unforgettable Experiences: Whether it’s enjoying an evening by the fireplace or a quiet moment in our rose garden, every detail at The Velvet Rose Inn is curated for your delight.

GOT QUESTIONS?

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Check-out is at 11:00 AM. If you need an early check-in or late check-out, please let us know in advance, and we’ll do our best to accommodate you.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer free on-site parking for all our guests.

Do you allow pets?

We love pets, but to ensure the comfort of all our guests, we do not allow pets at this time.

Can I host an event at The Velvet Rose Inn?

We’d love to help you celebrate special occasions. Please contact us directly to discuss event options and availability.

Are there attractions nearby?

Absolutely! We’re located close to a charming town square, nature trails and historic sites. We’re happy to recommend activities to make your stay extra special.
